Undergraduate Project:



During my undergraduate degree I researched methods of implementing parametric modelling in the design and manufacture of acoustic diffusers.

Below are a number of diffusers modelled in Rhino3D; calculation of PRD, and QRD, and BAD algorithms were automated for each respective diffuser type.

This approach allows for instant ‘remodelling’ of the diffuser based upon user-specified inputs such as the maximum well-depth, well-width, and total number of wells; these properties determine the acoustic characteristics of each diffuser.

A MATLAB Application was created to automate calculation of the PRD algorithm, from which the resultant series of well-depth values is exported to Grasshopper via a .csv file.

Automation of the QRD algorithm was performed entirely within Grasshopper.

A number of these were machined through various rapid-prototyping technologies, including CNC Milling, FDM 3D Printing and Laser Cutting
